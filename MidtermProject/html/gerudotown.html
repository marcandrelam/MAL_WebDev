Gerudo Town

Located far off in the center of the Gerudo Desert, Gerudo Town is a large vibrant community and home to the Gerudo people, who are all female. The Gerudo are fiercely protective of their culture, and do not allow a male (voe) into their town. Only females (vai) are allowed entry. The Daqo Chisay Shrine resides right outside Gerudo Town's walls. Gerudo Town also houses a Goddess Statue near the Southeast Gate.

Regional Food: Fresh Fruit from Lorn's Fruit Stand
