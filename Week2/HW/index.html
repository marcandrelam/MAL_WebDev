Ice Cream Sundae

Ice cream is the perfect dish for any time of the day. Eat it at work, home, the subway, or the park.

Ingredients

  • ice cream of choice
  • syrup of choice
  • whipped cream
  • whatever else you think of
  1. scoop ice cream into bowl
  2. put syrup on it
  3. put whipped cream if you wish
  4. add everything else you want
  5. eat
